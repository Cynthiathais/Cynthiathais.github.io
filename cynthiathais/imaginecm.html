Imagine Children's Museum

View Presentation

Description
Our team conducted ethnographic research examine the visitor experience at Imagine Children's Museum in Everett, WA to confirm the smartphone app’s desirability. Through our research findings, we were inspired by the thought of using ethnographic research to delve into how Interactive Media Design extended to non-digital spaces. The Imagine Children’s Museum pitch allowed them an opportunity to look at how to bring technology and innovation into a space in mindful and specific ways.
